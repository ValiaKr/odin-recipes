Tyrokafteri

Hint: Tyro=Cheese and Kafteri=Hot

I present you with yet another dip. Greeks loooove their dips. As stated in the subtitle, Tyrokafteri literally means hot cheese, so you know what you're getting into. The name sometimes changes from region to region. In Crete, for example, will call it kopanisti. But, personally, I find the name Tyrokafteri better, because it tells you what to expect!

Ingredients

  • 400gr feta cheese
  • Roasted bell peppers
  • 200gr greek yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Red chili flakes (the amount you'll use it's your choice)
  • 50gr extra virgin olive oil

Instructions

  1. Chop the roasted peppers and put them in a blender. Add in crumbled feta pieces along with lemon juice, red wine vinegar and red chili flakes. Pulse a few times.
  2. Add in greek yogurt and pulse a few more times.
  3. Stir in extra virgin olive oil and give it a final whirl.
  4. Lastly, taste and adjust the spice by adding more lemon juice or chili flakes.
