Frappe

(Instant iced coffee)

Before I share the recipe, let me say this:

As a Greek, who drinks at least one glass a day, the best result is ALWAYS achieved by hand. You can, of course, use an electric wheezer or whatever, but I don't recommend it. Just use a standard cocktail shaker and shake by hand!

Now, let's talk variations:

  • "Sketos": plain, no sugar
  • "Metrios": medium sweetness, use equal amount of coffee and sugar
  • "Glykos": sweet, the sougar is double the amount of the coffee

To all of theese variations you can add 1 tbsp of evaporated milk. I don't recommend it, though.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 tsp instant coffee
  • sugar (unless you're going for sketos)
  • 3-4 ice cubes
  • 1 glassful of cold water

Instructions

  1. In the shaker, add 2-3 tsp of cold water. (You want just enough to cover the coffee.) Add coffee, sugar and the ice cubes.
  2. Shake until a thick, creamy foam is produced. If your hand starts to hurt, work through the pain. Frappe is for the strong!!! Also, the foam is very, very, very important! You have to make it just right!
  3. Pour in a tall glass, fill up with cold water an serve with a drinking straw.
